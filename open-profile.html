This is your unique digital identity number. Everything you see on this page comes directly from information your device is already broadcasting to the internet.

Websites, governments and large companies can collect and store this data to build detailed profiles of your online behaviour.

A single 1×1 invisible pixel can be placed inside emails, websites or adverts. When you load a page or open an email containing it, the visit is recorded along with technical details about your device and connection. No cookies are required.

This is legal, widely used and almost impossible to notice.

To be clear: I am not storing any of your data and I will not remember your visit. This page exists purely to show you what is visible — and to make you aware.
