About

The person behind the screen.

Hey, I'm Leon. I build games, apps, robots, and random experiments just to see what's possible.

This site is where I put all of it — the finished stuff, the works-in-progress, and the things that probably shouldn't exist.

I'm from Yorkshire, UK, and mostly self-taught, always tinkering and learning by doing. Currently a student, I'm passionate about turning ideas into interactive experiences and hope to become a game developer. My creativity has been recognised a couple of times too — I'm a two-time BAFTA Young Game Designers game concept finalist.

If it's curious, playful, or a little chaotic, there's a good chance you'll find it here.
